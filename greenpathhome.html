GreenPath

Earn rewards for greener everyday travel while helping cities and companies measure real climate impact.

Explore the Idea

Main Features

Shows the greenest route based on carbon saved, time, and cost.
Rewards every kilometer walked, biked, or traveled by transit.
Redeem points for metro credits, cafés, bike rentals, and local offers.
Tracks CO₂ saved and personal environmental impact.
Adds streaks, city challenges, and leaderboards for habit formation.

Why This Model Is Different

GreenPath converts verified low-carbon trips into micro-carbon credits that companies can buy for ESG and Scope 3 reporting, funding user rewards in return.

How Value Flows

User chooses sustainable route
Trip verified through mobility data
Carbon savings calculated
Reward points issued
Corporate ESG funding supports rewards

Business Impact

GreenPath creates a B2B-funded reward ecosystem where commuters benefit from incentives, while companies receive measurable sustainability dashboards and localized climate action reporting.
